Powering Precision, Ensuring Performance – Welcome to Jai-Hind High Vacuum Pumps

As a leading name in the world of high-vacuum technology, we specialize in a premium selection of rotary vane type vacuum pumps and diffusion pumps, built to meet the ever-evolving needs of modern industry.

🌀 Precision-Engineered Vacuum Solutions

Our crown jewels? The oil-sealed high vacuum pumps, available in both direct drive and belt drive configurations. Whether you're running a cutting-edge research lab or a high-demand manufacturing line, our pumps are engineered to deliver consistent, reliable vacuum performance—day in, day out.

  • Direct Drive Pumps: Known for their high efficiency, lower noise, and compact design—perfect for clients who demand speed, simplicity, and seamless performance.
  • Belt Drive Pumps: Designed with flexibility in mind, ideal for setups requiring variable speeds and precise control.
